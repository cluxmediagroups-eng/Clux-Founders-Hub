Frequently Asked Questions

Questions?
Answered.

Is the workspace really free? What is the catch?
Yes — completely free for all 10 selected businesses. Workspace for the founder and one staff member costs nothing monthly. The only payment is the ₦10,000 one-time screening fee — paid before your application is reviewed, not for access.
Why is there a ₦10,000 screening fee?
It is a seriousness filter — not payment for workspace access. It ensures every application we review comes from a genuinely committed founder, and protects the quality of the ecosystem for everyone inside it.
What happens after I pay the screening fee?
Immediately after payment, you are directed to a detailed screening form on this page. After submitting, our team reviews your responses within 48–72 hours. Shortlisted founders receive an email invitation for a brief physical screening.
Is the screening fee refundable if I am not selected?
The ₦10,000 screening fee is non-refundable. It covers the cost of application review, regardless of outcome — consistent with how serious selection processes work.
Can early-stage businesses apply?
We require businesses to be already generating revenue. Purely idea-stage businesses are not eligible for this cohort. However, early-stage businesses with active customers and real operations are strongly encouraged to apply.
What does a day inside the ecosystem look like?
Members work from their dedicated desks alongside other serious founders in a professional, productive environment. Regular activities include monthly founder sessions. Beyond that — come in, build your business, and leverage the network.
How long is the ecosystem membership?
Membership is reviewed every 3–6 months. Renewal is not automatic — it requires continued activity, professional conduct, and ecosystem contribution. This keeps the environment dynamic and high-quality.
Where exactly is Clux Founders Hub?
We are in Langbasa, Ajah, Lagos. Full address and access details are shared with shortlisted applicants. Accessible from Lekki, Victoria Island, Ajah, Sangotedo, and surrounding areas.
